Admission Overview

Shree Rastriya Mavi School offers a comprehensive and engaging educational experience for students from kindergarten through 12th grade. Our curriculum is designed to challenge and inspire students, fostering a lifelong love of learning. We seek students who are curious, motivated, and eager to contribute to our diverse community.

Eligibility Criteria

Kindergarten

Must be 5 years old by September 1st of the year applying.

Grades 1-5

Must have successfully completed the previous grade level.

Grades 6-8

Must have a strong academic record and demonstrate commitment to learning.

Grades 9-10

Must have a minimum GPA of 2.0 and submit standardized test scores.

Application Process

Submit Application

Complete the online application form and submit all required documents including transcripts, recommendations, and personal statement.

Attend Entrance Exam

Participate in Entrance Exam with others and secure your spot!

Receive Decision

Admissions decisions will be communicated via Facebook within the specified timeframe.

Complete Enrollment

Upon acceptance, complete the enrollment process, submit required forms, and secure your spot in our community.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the average class size?
The average class size at Shree Rastriya Mavi School is 20 students, ensuring personalized attention and a supportive learning environment where every student can thrive.
What extracurricular activities are available?
We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports teams, music ensembles, drama club, debate team, robotics club, community service projects, and various academic competitions.
How can I learn more about the school?
You can attend one of our open houses, schedule a campus tour, contact our admissions office, or explore our website and social media channels for the latest updates and information.
What financial aid options are available?
We offer various financial aid options including merit-based scholarships, need-based assistance, and payment plans to make quality education accessible to deserving students.
